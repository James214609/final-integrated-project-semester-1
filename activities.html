Activities Heading

There's so much cool things for you and your special one to enjoy here.

At the Couples Resort, we have many romantic and energetic activities for couples to enjoy. We're located in Algonquin Park, adjacent to the lake, with swimming pools, boat rentals, a multisport court and in the winter, snowshoeing and icehiking.

Canoeing

Kayaks & Canoes

An important form of transportation for the Algonquin natives has always been the use of canoes on the beautiful lakes and rivers. Canoes and kayaks permit you to travel deep into Algonquin Park on our Galeairy Lake still as pristine as ever

Inquire at Sport Centre to go out super early next day (as early as sunrise)

Hiking and Cycling

Hiking & Biking

The Couples Resort Hiking Trails - On-site scenic hiking trails, leading to a scenic view of Algonquin Park, consisting of three loops, Lookout Trail 1.0 km in length, Bear trail loop 2.7 and Rail Line loop 1.2

Trail Map & Directions Available from sport center or front desk

Algonquin Park's interpretive walking trails are a great location to observe spring wildflowers.

The Couples Resort offers bike rentals as well as bike maps for four trails. Each of the trails are rated easy for all to enjoy.

Winter Activities

Winter Activities

Icehiking & Skating

Ice Hiking

Guaranteed Daily Winter Sport for you to enjoy nature! Touching Wildlife.

Fun and Easy and Safe. Invented Here in 2018. Ice Hiking is a Guaranteed Winter Sport, We guarantee it daily - 7 days a week, All Winter long, November to April. All Algonquin Park trails will now be easy to access even now in the Winter!

Ice Skating

Authentic Canadian Experience of Skating Outdoors:

  1. 1. In Algonquin Park at Mew Lake Campground, rough surface like the "good ol days".
  2. 2. At the Township Rink, a short 15 minute walk into the Village, general skating or skirmish hockey (all invited).

Provided Free to Use:

  • -Ice Skates up to size 14
  • -Mew Lake Park Pass from our Office

Cross-Contry Skiing and Snowshoeing

Skiing

Tranquil with peacefulness. Algonquin Park has International quality ski trails; track set and skating lane.

48 kilometres of groomed and 23 kilometres of non-groomed cross-country ski trails available in three low-usage trail networks, typically December to early April. Check the latest ski trail conditions. If you cannot go skiing you can always go IceHiking.

Provided Free to use:

  • - Wax-less cross-country skis, boots and poles.
  • - Waxing station for those that enjoy waxing.
  • - Park Pass from our Office
  • - Pro snowshoes
  • - Park Pass from our Office

Serene spots for Snowshoeing:

  1. 1) Algonquin outdoor Logging Museum Trail
  2. 2) Spruce Bog Boardwalk Trail
  3. 3) Mew Lake Old Railway Trail Go to Mew Lake Campground for starting point.
